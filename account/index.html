Help & FAQ

Quick answers for shoppers and independent store owners in Greater Manchester.

Customer FAQ

How do I pay?

You pay securely by card via Stripe when you checkout from a live store — Apple Pay and Google Pay show when supported.

Delivery and collection?

Each store chooses collection and local delivery rules. Availability is shown on the store page before you pay.

Where does Clip Services operate?

Right now Clip Services lists only live onboarded stores in Greater Manchester.

I don’t see my basket on another device

Carts save per browser or phone for faster checkout without forcing a login mid-shop. Signed-in customers see linked baskets listed here.

Store owner FAQ

How do I join?

Apply. After approval you can publish products from the owner dashboard.

Sale prices and weight-based items

Set a usual price plus an optional sale price in the dashboard. Choose sold by weight (per kg etc.) — your storefront shows SALE and unit pricing.

Payouts and fees

Stripe handles card payments per order. Fees and payouts follow your Stripe Connect setup linked from the dashboard.
